Small donations. Real names. Visible impact.

In many underserved communities in India, a treatable condition becomes a life-threatening one simply because a family can't spare a few hundred rupees a month. That's often less than the cost of a lunch here.

We don't believe charity should feel like dropping money into a void. Every campaign on our donation page is a specific person with a specific need — a grandmother who needs blood pressure medication, a kid who needs an inhaler. You can see their story, watch their funding bar fill up, and know when they've been helped.
